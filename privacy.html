Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026  ·  Effective: June 9, 2026

Short version: Clingogo extensions run entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your browsing data, personal information, or usage history. Ever.

1. Who We Are

Clingogo is an independent Chrome extension studio operated by Do Viet Quang, based in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. We build and maintain browser extensions distributed through the Chrome Web Store.

If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at support@clingogo.com.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Our extensions are designed with privacy as a default. We do not collect:

  • Your browsing history or the URLs you visit
  • Personal identifying information (name, email, location)
  • Keystrokes, form inputs, or clipboard contents
  • Device identifiers or IP addresses
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data

3. Information We Do Collect (Subscriptions Only)

If you purchase a Pro subscription, payment is processed by Polar (polar.sh), our payment provider. Polar acts as the Merchant of Record and collects:

  • Your email address (to issue a license key and send receipts)
  • Payment information (handled entirely by Polar — we never see your card details)
  • Country of residence (for tax compliance purposes)

We receive only your email address and license key status from Polar, solely to verify your subscription. Polar's privacy policy applies to payment data: polar.sh/legal/privacy.

4. Local Storage

Our extensions may store your preferences (such as brightness settings, per-site theme choices, or on/off state) locally in your browser using Chrome's storage.local or storage.sync API. This data:

  • Never leaves your device (except sync data, which goes through your own Google account)
  • Is only used to remember your settings between sessions
  • Can be cleared at any time by uninstalling the extension

5. Permissions

Our extensions request only the permissions necessary to function. For example, a dark mode extension requires access to page content to apply CSS — this access is used solely to render the dark theme and is never logged or transmitted.

We will always explain in the Chrome Web Store listing why each permission is needed.

6. Third-Party Services

Our extensions do not embed third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking pixels. The only third-party interaction is:

  • Polar — for license key validation on Pro features (network request to verify your subscription status)
  • Google Fonts — loaded on our website (clingogo.com), not inside extensions

7. Children's Privacy

Our extensions and services are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us immediately at support@clingogo.com.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of our extensions after any changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

9. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or requests:

  • Website: clingogo.com
