Features

Everything dark mode
should have been

🧠

AI Color Analysis

LunaMode reads the page's actual color intent — brand colors, backgrounds, accents — and remaps them to a dark palette that looks hand-crafted.

⚡

Zero-lag Performance

Injects a single stylesheet per domain. No JS running per-frame, no filter layers on every element. Pages load and scroll at full speed.

🎨

Per-site Theming

Save different brightness, contrast, and warmth settings per domain. Your news site, your code editor, and your email can each feel exactly right.

🌅

Sunrise / Sunset Schedule

Enable dark mode automatically based on your location's actual sunrise and sunset times — no manual clock to set and forget.

🔄

Cloud Sync

All your site preferences and settings live in the cloud. Open a new browser or a new machine and everything is exactly where you left it.

🛡️

Zero Data Collection

LunaMode never sees what sites you visit. Color analysis runs entirely on-device. Your browsing is yours alone.
